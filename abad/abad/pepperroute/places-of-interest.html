      Abad Pepper Route is a quaint colonial looking hotel in Fort Kochi. Located very close to Fort Kochi Beach, it is the perfect getaway to explore Mattanchery and Fort Kochi. It is at a walkable distance from all major tourist spots in Fort Kochi. We offer facilities like concierge, travel assistance and airport pickup.
