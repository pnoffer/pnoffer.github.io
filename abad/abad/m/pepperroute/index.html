Abad Pepper Route, Fort Kochi

An Affordable And Cosy Stay In Abad Pepper Route, Fort Kochi

Snuggled in the vintage-looking town of Fort Kochi, Abad Pepper Route is a reminder of the colonial era beauty of Kochi. Pocket friendly with all essential amenities and services, Abad Pepper Route is perfect for travellers wanting a comfortable yet affordable stay. Located Just 500 metres away from St Francis Church and Fort Kochi Beach, Pepper Route is a bed and breakfast hotel, and a true gateway to explore the cultural heritage of Mattanchery and Fort Kochi.

Abad Pepper Route is a budget hotel in Fort Kochi suitable for groups, couples and families. With clean and comfortable rooms, breakfast room, concierge, and travel assistance, we are a perfect stay option for all travellers. The hotel offers English breakfast. It also offers luggage storage facility, airport shuttle service and an exclusive tour desk to help guests organize trips to tourist spots. Abad Pepper Route is 32 km from Kochi International Airport, 10 km from Railway Station, 250 metres from Fort Kochi Bus Stand and just 3 km away from Kochi Sea Port. This budget hotel in Fort Kochi is an ideal base for exploring Kochi's exciting spots like the Mattancherry Palace, Chinese Fishing Nets and the Jewish Synagogue!

Accommodation At Abad Pepper Route, Fort Kochi

 

Abad Pepper Route, Fort Kochi, offers great accommodation at an affordable price. These rooms are spread across 200 sq. ft. and are fully air-conditioned. They have comfortable linens, a wardrobe, and either a large bed or a twin bed for you to rest. These rooms have a TV, complimentary Wi-Fi access, and laundry service. Have a peaceful stay at Abad!
