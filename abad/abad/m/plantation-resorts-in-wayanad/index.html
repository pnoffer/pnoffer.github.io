Parisons Plantation Experiences by Abad- A Luxury Resort in Wayanad

Experience a Luxurious Rustic Charm at One of the Top Resorts in Wayanad

Parisons Plantation Experience is a 200-year-old property nestled amidst the lush green tea estates in Wayanad. Our luxury resorts in Wayanad retains the rustic charm of a bygone era and is elegantly coupled with modern features to help our guests enjoy kick back and relax from their usual routines. It is located about 60 kilometres from the Kannur International Airport and has an average travel time of 1 hour and 45 minutes. This luxury resort in Wayanad is also in close proximity to Kozhikode Railway Station and Kalpetta Bus Stand. Guests who visit our hotel can stay in the Estate Heritage Bungalow or the Estate Boutique Bungalow, both of which are colonial structures that offer a perfect sojourn for the discerning traveller. Our resorts in Wayanad also accommodate a multicuisine restaurant where a lavish spread consisting of both traditional and international dishes are prepared for our guests. We also have a spa where a variety of massages and treatments are offered to help you relax and rejuvenate yourself. Guests can visit the library or indulge in other activities like plantation walks, cycling, and bird watching at Wayanad's best resort. 

Enjoy An Opulent Stay Amidst Lush Green Tea Estates

Parisons Plantation Experiences by Abad is one of the best Mananthavady resorts that promises an opulent stay. Guests who visit our luxury resort in Wayanad can enjoy a pleasant stay at our Estate Heritage Bungalow or the Estate Boutique Bungalow. Both of these Bungalows at our Wayanad resort are spacious and come with a fireplace and an array of modern amenities. These rooms in our hill top resorts in Wayanad, Kerala can accommodate 3 adults and 2 children comfortably. There are six Heritage Bungalow and four Boutique Bungalow rooms. The Heritage Bungalow rooms overlook the swimming pool and the tea estates. Boutique Bungalows come with a common verandah area where you can enjoy the intricacies of nature. Our bungalows are the best place to stay in Wayanad to enjoy its serenity!

Relish A Confluence Of Flavours

Parisons Plantation Experiences by Abad is one of the best resorts in Wayanad that also has an in-house restaurant where our guests can enjoy a myriad of flavours from across the world. Our restaurant in Wayanad offers pleasant views of the tea estates that surround our resort and exudes a warm and welcoming vibe. The ingredients that we use for all our meals are freshly sourced from nearby markets and our experienced chefs work their magic on them to give them a rich flavour. Additionally, our friendly staff will take care of all your needs and help you enjoy a memorable dining experience.
