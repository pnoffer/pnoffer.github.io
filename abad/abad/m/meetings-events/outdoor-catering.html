Outdoor Catering

Looking for a sumptuous and lavish feast, that is well-organized and reliable? Look no further than Abad Hotels! We have a rich history spanning four decades and have come to be known to provide hospitality and catering services of the highest quality over the years, serving at both corporate and social events.
You may choose between a fixed menu or the more popular buffet option, as per your convenience. Our food is prepared in hygienic conditions with the highest safety standards, we put a strong onus on the quality of the food that we serve. Thus, our guests are treated to foods of the highest culinary standards. Additionally, Abad Hotels are spread across the length and breadth of Kerala, enabling us to cater to you at ease, anywhere across the state. From company offsites to business events & conferences, weddings, or just a large family gathering; we cater to events of every kind. You wish, we serve!

 

Fixed Menu

 

Abad Hotels provides specially curated menus, with a fixed number of items. We serve a limited number of dishes that are pre-plated and served at the table. This menu is ideal for smaller gatherings such as cocktail parties, family get-togethers, business meetings, office celebrations etc. To see our fixed menus, please click the button below.

Buffets


Abad Hotels also provides buffet options for breakfast, lunch, hi-tea and dinner spreads. The menu can be customized according to your requirement, you can opt for a few dishes to be served at breakfast or a more lavish spread for grand celebrations like weddings. Live Counters

 

We also set up live counters serving up snacks and other delicacies. You can pick and choose the dishes of your choice and our chefs will ensure that the dishes are perfectly prepared according to your varied tastes. This setup is perfect for informal gatherings, kids parties as well as kitty parties. Chaat, icecream, omelette counters and salad bars are the more popular options that we cater to.
