Abad Cruise on the Lake, Alleppey

Set Sail to Happiness at Abad Cruise On The Lake

Houseboats in Alleppey have emerged as a unique product of Kerala. With Abad Cruise on the Lake, you get the opportunity to stay and explore the vast lakes and backwaters of Alleppy, and experience life of the local people on the shores. Our houseboats offer modern amenities and delicious food that is cooked and served on board. Our luxury houseboats in Kerala are located just 1.3 Km from the Nehru Trophy Finishing Point. We're comfortably located, about 3.2 kilometres from Alappuzha Bus Stand and 6.4 km from the Railway Station. These premium houseboats have the aesthetics of a traditional Kettuvallam and are powered with modern amenities to provide our guests with the most comfortable and authentic Kerala experience. Moreover, the steel hull ensures that these houseboats are very safe for our guests. We have also used eco-friendly materials like bamboo and coir so that the beauty of the scenic backwaters remain uncontaminated.

Our premium Alleppey houseboats' timing starts from 12:00 pm till 9:00 am. You can enjoy cruising through the backwaters from 12:00 pm to 5.00 pm and from 7:00 am to 9:00 am. As you sail through the backwaters of Alleppey gazing at the lush green paddy fields and serene watery villages of Alleppey, you are soaked into a world of beauty and grace. You can also take delight in local activities like fishing in the backwater with villagers, enjoy local shopping and experience a glimpse of backwater villages of Kerala. 
 
Enjoy the play of colours in the sky during the sunrise and sunset as you luxuriate in your houseboat in Alleppey. 

Accommodation at Abad Cruise on the Lake, Alleppey

Our Alappuzha houseboats are a standing testament to the combined beauty of new world luxury and vintage feel! Traverse through the canals of Alappuzha and bask in the beauty of Kerala and its exotic nature! We have two types of houseboats for differing requirements. Our one-bedroom accommodation is 800 sq. ft. in size and can easily facilitate 2 adults and 2 children. The two-bedroom accommodation is spread across 1100 sq. ft., and can house 4 adults and 4 children comfortably. Our topline accommodation. All our Houseboats in Kerala are completely air-conditioned and fitted with TVs. Guests also have access to wardrobes for their utility.

Come to Abad and truly experience Alleppey!

Dine on a Scrumptious Meal

Satiate your hunger with delicious food cooked on board. Our Alleppey Houseboats are decked with a fully equipped kitchen and we cook all three meals of the day for you. Abad takes care of your food cravings by serving you scrumptious meals. Served with quality, taste and satisfaction of the guests in mind, our chefs whip up a mouth-watering spread and serve you aboard the deck. Sail through the backwaters of Alleppey in our houseboats, while feasting on your favourite dish!
