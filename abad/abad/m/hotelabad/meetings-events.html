Meetings & Events at Abad Fort Kochi

Banquet Hall

Abad Fort Kochi houses a 3000 sq. ft. banquet hall that can accommodate anywhere between 250 -300 people comfortably. Medium-sized events like weddings, product launches, anniversaries, birthday parties and more can be held at this banquet hall in Cochin.

Boardroom

Abad Fort Kochi has a boardroom that is best suited for hosting formal events like sales meetings, annual review meetings, boardroom discussions and more. Our boardroom in Cochin can accommodate between 20 -25 people and come with a range of amenities like air-conditioning, projector and high-speed Wi-Fi to help you have a swell event experience.
