Couples Guide to Kerala

Kerala, God’s own country, is one of the most fascinating places to visit, especially for couples! Newlyweds love to spend their honeymoon in Kerala due to its seductive ambiance. The pristine backwaters, the lush greenery and wildlife, and the concept of getting lost in the culture make it one of the best places for couples.

Honeymoon Packages by Abad

Abad Hotels is one of the best honeymoon resorts in Kerala that offers some exciting and riveting honeymoon packages for couples. In God’s own country, places like Alleppey, Cochin, Thekkady, Munnar, and Kumarakom are the most anticipated destinations amongst newlyweds. And thanks to Abad Hotels, you can now enjoy your quality time with your partner at these honeymoon places in Kerala in the most luxurious resorts!

Abad Atrium Hotel, Cochin

Cochin is one of the most romantic destinations in Kerala. It is known for its beige beaches and scintillating views. The mesmerising seascape and candlelight dinners by the beach during sunsets are enough to sweep you off your feet!

Abad Atrium Hotel is one of the finest honeymoon resorts in Cochin that offers the perfect honeymoon package for couples. It provides a one-night romantic stay at the resort’s Superior Room. It lets you spend quality time with your loved one and share a night of pure bliss and comfort.

Abad Copper Castle, Munnar

Known as the Kashmir of the South, Munnar is pivotal to honeymoon packages! Surrounded by crystal clear waterfalls and lush mountains, Munnar is one of the most breathtaking heavenly places to go for honeymooning. It's tempting scenery and exotic wildlife skips the heart beat of every couple. The dainty hill station carefully sits amid tea and coffee plantations, which makes the climate and the sight pleasant and spectacular.

Abad Copper Castle is a luxury resort, which finds itself nestled in the heart of Munnar. Its beautiful placement allows occupants with awe-inspiring views of the tea estates on one side and lavish valley beds on the other. It is the perfect little pill for newlyweds to enjoy their honeymoon! It is one of the finest honeymoon resorts in Kerala that offers two nights to couples at its non AC Superior Rooms. The luxurious property also lets you enjoy a nice, intimate time with your partner by the campfires.

Abad Green Forest, Thekkady

Thekkady is another enticing land for couples to spend their honeymoon. It is the most unconventional honeymoon destination, which paves the way to your partner’s heart! Known for its abundance of spice gardens and picturesque sceneries, Thekkady greets couples with the most welcoming arms. It is a quintessential place for couples to relax, unwind, and communicate.

Abad Green Forest offers myriad romantic activities for couples to engage in. The resort in Thekkady provides an Ayurvedic centre. A couple’s spa is one of the spiciest ingredients for making the best honeymoon experience! The luxurious property also lets you take calming nature walks and cosy boat rides across Periyar Lake. It also offers honeymoon packages that lets you and your loved one spend two unperturbed nights at its non AC Jungle Rooms!

Abad Whispering Palms, Kumarakom

Kumarakom is an amorous destination for couples that is characterised by its quixotic boathouses! This is the place that gave birth to romantic Kerala boathouse honeymoon package offers for couples. Nothing screams more romantic than staying in private boathouses on the pristine backwaters surrounded by the beautiful, almighty nature. Kumarakom is the ultimate getaway for couples to get involved romantically.

Abad Whispering Palms provides a plethora of delightful activities for partners. Couples can enjoy bird-watching, hit the ayurvedic spa, or even cycle amid the calming nature. The resort in Kumarakom also lets newlyweds unwind and spend a passionate night in its Garden View Cottages.

Abad Turtle Beach Resort, Alleppey

Like Kumarakom, Alleppey is another great place to experience the serenity and beauty of backwaters. It is a romantic destination where you and your partner can go on intimate boat rides and explore the sun-kissed beaches with encapsulating sunset views. There are multiple luxurious resorts in Alleppey that let you enjoy cruises on houseboats and ride the backwaters.

If you and your loved one are planning to visit Alleppey, then make sure to take advantage of the romantic packages offered by the lavish Abad Turtle Beach Resort! It bestows couples with a night at its Beach Cottages, promising an unforgettable journey.

So, what are you waiting for? Pack your bags and set foot on your romantic trip to Kerala!
