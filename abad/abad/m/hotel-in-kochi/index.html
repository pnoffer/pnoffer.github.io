Staycation At Abad Plaza in Kochi

Abad Plaza - A 3 Star Hotel in Central Kochi

Immaculate Blend of Comfort & Luxury at our Kochi Hotels

Abad Plaza is a signature 3-star hotel located in Central Kochi that offers a perfect sojourn for the discerning business traveller. Our hotel on MG Road Kochi is located about 40 minutes from the Cochin International Airport and about 5 minutes from the Ernakulam North & South Railway Station. Guests who visit our hotel can choose to stay at our Superior Rooms and Suites which are aesthetically pleasing and come with stylish interiors. We also have two in-house restaurants viz. Regency and Kadaloram. A quaint coffee shop called Canopy is also present within our Kochi hotel where you can grab quick bites. If you are in the mood to taste a variety of cakes, pastries, and cookies, you can head over to the Patisserie - a bakery present within our hotel. Additionally, we also have seven banquet halls and one exhibition hall which are perfect for hosting a variety of social and business events. 

We also have an in-house spa called Revival Ayurveda Centre where our guests can enjoy a variety of healing techniques. Our experienced masseuses and masseurs will help relieve all your stress and anxiety and help rejuvenate your mind and body. 

A Superb Stay Awaits You in Kochi

Guests who visit our hotel can choose to stay at our Superior Rooms and Suites. These living spaces are perfectly designed keeping in mind the requirements of modern travellers. Each of these living spaces come with comfortable beds and plush furniture and can accommodate 2 adults and 2 children with ease. A range of amenities like air-conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi laundry service, mini bar, 32-inch LED television, and 24-hour room service are available to help our guests have a memorable stay at our hotel in Ernakulam. 

Dig Into a Variety of Tastes at our Café & Restaurant in Kochi

Abad Plaza has two restaurants in Kochi within its premises namely the Regency and the Kadaloram restaurant. The Regency is a multicuisine restaurant that serves a variety of dishes from across the world onto your platter. Kadaloram is an authentic Kerala cuisine restaurant serving a range of ethnic dishes that will tantalize your tastebuds. We also have a coffee shop called Canopy, which operates around the clock and serves an array of snacks, drinks and dishes which are perfect for satiating your snacking needs. If you want to treat yourself to a variety of cakes, pastries and cookies, you can head over to the Patisserie where you can enjoy a variety of baked items and quench your hunger pangs. 


Come, enjoy a superb dining experience at our cafe and restaurants in Kochi. 

Meetings & Events

Abad Plaza has seven banquet halls and one exhibition hall that is perfect for hosting a variety of events. These banquet halls are spacious and come with a host of amenities like air-conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, overhead projector, and audiovisual equipment. Hotel in Kochi also offers catering services as per the menu and also have a team of dedicated professionals who will gladly assist you during your event to help you have a perfect experience.
