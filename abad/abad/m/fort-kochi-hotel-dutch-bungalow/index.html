Abad Dutch Bungalow - A Vintage Hotel in Fort Kochi

Timeless Dutch Architecture And Contemporary Living - A Heritage Hotel In Fort Kochi

Abad Dutch Bungalow is a 375-year-old heritage hotel in Fort Kochi. It was built in the year 1668 by the Dutch East India Company. It came to private possession in the year 1874 and it still retains the quaint yet rustic charm of the bygone days. Our heritage hotel is built using classic Dutch architecture which has successfully stood the test of time. It is located about 43 kilometres from the Cochin International Airport and can be reached in an hour's drive. Guests who visit our hotel can experience a resplendent stay at our Heritage Classic and Heritage Boutique rooms, both of which are spacious and come decked out with chic interiors and plush furniture. A splendid poolside restaurant called Food Mill is present within our premises where you can enjoy a myriad of delicacies freshly prepared by our experienced chefs. 

Enjoy A Cozy Stay

Guests who visit our heritage hotel in Fort Kochi can enjoy a splendid stay at our Heritage Classic and Heritage Boutique rooms. Both these rooms are spacious and have a separate living area where you can set your feet and relax after a long day. Our rooms in Fort Kochi come decked out with chic interiors, plush furniture and comfortable beds. Additionally, our guests can enjoy a range of amenities like air-conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, and laundry service when they stay at our Heritage hotel. 

Tantalize Your Tastebuds

Abad Dutch Bungalow has an in-house restaurant called Food Mill where our guests can enjoy a range of multicuisine delicacies from across the world. Our restaurant near Fort Kochi Beach is famous for its many fish-based-delicacies. Different type of fishes are freshly sourced from the nearby Fort Kochi Beach and our experienced chefs add a tinge of their magic on it to give it a rich, flavourful taste. Our restaurant is situated right next to our indoor swimming pool, enjoy a melange of traditional and international tastes when you visit our restaurant in Fort Kochi.
