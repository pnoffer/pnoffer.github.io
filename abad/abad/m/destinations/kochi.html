Explore Kochi

Kochi features a network of backwaters which is the main reason why many travellers visit the city. This city features many waterways that connect various small villages. If you plan to spend some quality time exploring the backwaters you could hire a houseboat from Alleppey and that should do the trick. It also features a panoramic skyline, busy marketplaces and much more as it is one of the most populated cities in Kerala. Kochi features some of the best Kerala resorts that are perfect for both business and leisure travellers. Come, visit this port city during your next trip to Kerala.


Abad Dutch Bungalow

Abad Dutch Bungalow is one of the finest hotels in Kochi. It is a heritage hotel that has a history of close to four centuries. This hotel is built using timeless Dutch architecture and its rooms feature chic interiors, wooden floors and many other amenities to help the guests have a wonderful stay in Kerala. It is in close proximity to Jew Town, Dutch Palace, Santa Cruz Basilica and much more.


Abad Pepper Route

Abad Pepper Route is a budget hotel that helps you stay in the midst of Kochi at pocket-friendly prices. The rooms at this hotel in Kochi come decked with modern amenities, comfortable beds, linens and more to help you stay comfortably. It is located just 500 metres from popular attractions such as Fort Kochi Beach, St. Francis Church and many other places of interest.


Abad Metro

Abad Metro is a contemporary hotel that is located in close proximity to major travel hubs in Kochi. This budget hotel in Kochi features comfortable rooms that come equipped with modern amenities such as air-conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, a wardrobe and much more to help you have a memorable stay. The hotel is located close to places of interest such as Hill Palace Museum, Chottanikkara Temple and other important attractions.


Abad Atrium

Abad Atrium is a luxury hotel in Kochi that provides a perfect sojourn for both business and leisure travellers. Our hotel rooms come decked with modern amenities such as air conditioning, complimentary breakfast, 24-hour room service and more to help enable a comfortable stay in Kerala. Some of the places close to our hotel include Chinese Fishing Nets, Hill Palace Museum and much more.


Abad Plaza

Abad Plaza is a 3-star hotel in Kochi that is the ideal destination for the discerning business traveller. This hotel is located in close proximity to important attractions such as Marine Drive, Jewish Synagogue, Dutch Palace.


Abad Fort

Abad Fort is a picturesque beachside hotel in Kochi that offers a premium stay to modern travellers. Our hotel is in close proximity to spice markets and a steady aroma of these spices fills the air around our property. Some of the closest places of interest around our hotel include Fort Kochi Beach, Chinese Fishing Nets and Vasco Da Gama square.
