Abad Hotels & Resorts Privilege Membership Card

 
Enquire Now!
 

The membership of ABAD Hotels & Resorts entitles the cardholder to the following benefits at any of the associated Hotels & Restaurants of its Group.

SUMMARY OF PRIVILEGE CARD BENEFITS

 

Discounts on Food

20% discount on food at all Restaurants of Abad Hotels & Resorts. (Except Canopy)
10% discount at Canopy – MG Road.
10% discount at the Food Court in Nucleus Mall.
 

Other Discounts and Benefits

20% discount on Beverages (non-alcoholic).
20% discount on in-room dining.
20% discount on Pastry Shop purchases. And 40% discount post 8pm.
20% discount on take away orders.
20% discount on food festivals.
25% discount at Boomerang at Abad Nucleus Mall.
25% discount at B-Activ at Abad Nucleus Mall.
20% discount on published rates for Spa.
20% discount on Best Available Rates for room reservations on weekdays* and 15% discount on Best Available Rates for room reservations on weekends*.
Upto a maximum of 15 guests on one table to avail the above discounts against one membership card
10% Discount at Wild Fish
 

Discount on Banqueting

10% discount on banqueting on published rates

  • The Membership term (validity period) is 12 calendar months from the date of issuance of the Membership.

  • All vouchers are transferable under this program. (Except for Banquet Certificate)
  • Compulsory swimming pool attire and club rules to be followed according to management, rules and regulations to be followed at all times during the time of usage of this facility. Not valid during special Promotions / Events.
  • The total amount of the Membership fee will be refunded provided the Membership has not been used and the refund is requested by the Member within 7 days of acceptance of Membership. The refund will only be actioned if there is a valid reason given in writing to the management and only after the verification and discretion by the management the same will be accepted.
  • In the event that the Membership Card or the certificates are lost, these cannot be replaced or the money refunded.
  • Fraud or abuse concerning the program membership is subject to appropriate administrative/or legal action by the Hotel, including immediate termination of the Membership.
  • In the event of any dispute, these will be settled through arbitration as per the Indian Arbitration Act at Ernakulum, Kochi.

  • Presentation of the Program Membership Card is imperative for availing the discounts and benefits.
  • Only one Card may be presented per table / per visit.
  • All discounts are subject to immediate clearance of bill payment. There shall be no carry forward / credit faculties that will be entertained while using this membership program.
  • All discounts and benefits are valid only while dinning at Abad Hotels & Resorts Restaurants.
  • Discounts cannot be given in conjunction with any other special promotion/discount program.
  • The value of the specific discount shall not include central, state, local taxes or off-premise
  • Children above 5 years of age are included in the total guest count to calculate discounts. Age 6-11 is considered on half Buffet charges and 12 and above is taken as an Adult.
  • If more than 3 persons in a room, extra bed will be compulsory on a chargeable basis of Rs.500/- as a discounted price for the member.
  • Any request on Eggless cake can be provided on an extra charge while using the complimentary certificate.
  • All reservations are subject to allocation availability. Room reservations must be made directly with the INDULGE Member’ Help Desk number that will be provided on the reverse of the vouchers. Any bookings made through Travel Agents, Corporations or Online are NOT eligible for reservations or discounts under this program. Room reservations under this Program are non-commissionable and need to be booked at least 48 hours prior. Amendments/cancellations of Room reservations must be made with a 24-hour prior notice or else the certificate will be taken as used.

  • A valid Govt. ID proof is mandatory to be produced at any time on demand by the Hotel In-charge (Manager on Duty).
