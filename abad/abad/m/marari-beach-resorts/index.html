Abad Turtle Beach Resort, Mararikulam

A Peaceful Getaway in Mararikulam - The Best Beach Resort in Alleppey

Abad Turtle Beach Resort in Mararikulam, is a beautiful beachfront resort that overlooks the white sandy beach and has a rivulet flowing on one side. The resort, located very close to the coastal village of Mararikulam, is spread across an area of 13 acres of land with 30 premium cottages. It is 72.8 km from Kochi Airport and 12.6 km from Alleppey Railway Station. This is one of the Alleppey's best resorts with a private beach in Marari where you can enjoy the peace of privacy and the glorious white sands without any disturbance.

The rooms at Abad Turtle Beach Resort are spacious, air-conditioned, and facilitated with en-suite bathrooms and individual patios. They are also equipped with a TV, telephone, fridge, and other modern amenities. The multi-cuisine restaurant offers continental, Indian, and regional cuisine and is located adjacent to the elevated swimming pool and the sunbathing deck. Turtle Beach Resort is the house to Revival Ayurveda Centre, the best spa in Alleppey where you can knead away your stress with Ayurvedic massages. This holiday beach resort in Alleppey allows you to indulge in various activities like cycling, fishing, indoor games, outdoor games, and many more. With natural ponds, a butterfly garden, and an organic vegetable farm, this is the best resort in Alleppey to spend your vacation!

Accommodations in Alleppey at Abad Turtle Beach Resort

With the breezy Arabian sea in the front, our Beach Cottages have the most scenic view and offers a serene stay in Mararikulam. These spacious cottages, that can comfortably accommodate 4 guests, are spread over an area of 400 sq. ft. These accommodations in Alleppey also have a balcony for you to relax and enjoy the soothing ambience of Abad! The rooms either have a large bed or twin beds, and a premium attached bathroom. Amenities such as Wi-Fi, A/C, and TV are provided to aid you during your stay!

Go on a Taste Tour!

Abad Turtle Beach Resort has one of the best multi-cuisine restaurants in Mararikulam! Our guests can enjoy a variety of dishes that are inspired by delicacies from around the world! Whether it is traditional North Indian, South Indian or continental foods, we make sure to serve you the best. We put the onus on quality, taste and satisfaction. We have our own organic farm in the resort from where we source natural and healthy ingredients. Do try our traditional Kerala dishes and treat your taste buds with our gourmet delicacies. You can also get your dish customised as per your wish!

Meetings and Events

Abad Turlte Beach Resort is home to one of the best banquet halls in Mararikulam. It is spread over an area of 1200 sq. ft., and can easily accommodate over a hundred people. With all necessary amenities like Wi-Fi, A/C, overhead projector and other audiovisual equipment, our banquet hall in Mararikulam is ideal for events like business parties, college events, conferences, birthday bashes and reunions! Come and conduct your events at one of the best resorts in Alleppey!
