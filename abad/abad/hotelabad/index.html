      Experience Comfort & Convenience at Abad Fort Kochi – One of the Best Hotels in Kochi

      Abad Fort Kochi is a picturesque seaside hotel located in the thriving metropolis of Cochin. Many spice markets are located in close proximity to our hotel and you can savour the authentic aroma that emanates from these places. A unique cobble-stoned path leads the way to the hotel and perfectly complements the surroundings. Our Fort Kochi hotel is located about 40 kilometres from Cochin International Airport and can be reached within an hour's drive. Guests who visit our hotel near Cochin Airport can choose to stay at our Deluxe Rooms or Suites, both of which are spacious and aesthetically pleasing. Our hotel in Kochi houses an in-house restaurant called Pavilion, which serves a range of multicuisine delicacies. An in-house coffee shop called Cafe Mattancherry is also present to quench your need for a quick bite and drink. Our hotel also features a 3000 sq. ft. banquet hall, which can accommodate up to 300 people and is perfectly suited to host medium to large events. For hosting smaller and more formal events, we have a 500 sq. ft. boardroom which can accommodate between 20 - 25 people and comes with a range of modern amenities to help you have a perfect event experience. 

      When staying at the best hotel in Fort Kochi, make time and visit the many places of historical importance like the Dutch Palace and the Hill Palace Museum. If you are looking to enjoy a spiritual experience, you can head to Chottanikkara Temple, St. Francis Church, and the Jewish Synagogue. Other places of interest like the Chinese Fishing Nets, Wonderla Amusement Park, Lulu Mall, and more are also present in close proximity to our hotel in Cochin. 

      Enjoy A Pleasant Stay At Abad Fort Kochi

      Abad Fort Kochi features two types of living spaces viz. Deluxe Rooms and Suites. The Deluxe Rooms and Suites at our hotel in Cochin are perfectly suited for both business and leisure travellers. These rooms in Kochi are spacious and can accommodate 3 adults and 1 child with ease. The suites have a separate living room that is complete with plush furniture where you can kick back and relax after a long day. Our air-conditioned rooms come with modern amenities like high-speed Wi-Fi, in-room safe, and wardrobe are also present within our Kochi hotel rooms.

      Indulge In Authentic Delicacies Of Kerala

      Abad Fort Kochi has an in-house restaurant called Pavilion and a coffee shop called the Cafe Mattancherry. The Pavilion is a fine dining restaurant that serves an array of tasty delicacies from across the world. At our restaurant in Cochin, we take pride in serving quality food that will stay etched in your memory for a long time. All our ingredients are sourced from nearby markets and our chefs work their magic on the ingredients to cater it to your unique tastes. When you visit our restaurant in Cochin be sure to ask for the various fish-based delicacies that we prepare as these are the speciality dishes at our restaurant. 

      Host A Variety Of Events At Our Banquet Halls

      Our Hotel in Cochin also features a banquet hall and a boardroom. The banquet hall is a 3000 sq. ft. structure that can accommodate anywhere between 250 -300 people.  It is perfect for hosting small to medium-sized events like marriages, product launches, anniversaries, get-togethers and more. The boardroom at our hotel can accommodate a crowd of about 25 people and is best suited for annual review meetings, sales meets, dealer meets and other similar events.
