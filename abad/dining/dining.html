    Restaurants of Abad

    Abad features multi-cuisine restaurants and cafes in its hotels at multiple locations. They cater to different requirements of our guests but also focus on the local cuisine experience to give our guests a taste of the land they're visiting! Our vegetables, meat and other raw materials come from the local market, thus helping vendors with their business and doing our part in giving back to society. 
    The multi-cuisine restaurants in our hotels serve a variety of dishes. We take pride in cooking what you need in the way you want it, and our focus lies in quality, taste and the satisfaction of our customers. Our welcoming ambience and friendly staff will ensure that your dining experience is a happy and relishable one!
