Abad Metro- A Contemporary Hotel In Central Kochi

Experience A Perfect Sojourn At Our Hotel in Central Kochi

Abad Metro is a contemporary hotel in Kochi that offers a comfortable stay with a plethora of modern amenities and offers excellent hospitality for both business and leisure travellers. Our hotel is located about 40 minutes from the Cochin International Airport and about 5 minutes from the Ernakulam North & South Railway Station. Guests who visit our hotel can choose to stay at one of our three rooms, viz. Standard Rooms, Deluxe Rooms & Suites. These living spaces are spacious and come with comfortable beds, plush furniture and a host of modern amenities. You can also host a variety of social and business events at the Summit Banquet Hall, which has an area of 600 sq. ft. and can accommodate up to 65 people.

Enjoy A Comfortable Stay

Guests who visit our hotel can choose to stay in our three living spaces viz. Standard Rooms, Deluxe Rooms & Suites. Both the rooms & suites are spacious and come with stylish and plush furniture. These rooms in Kochi can accommodate 2 adults and 2 children comfortably. They come with amenities such as air-conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, LED television, wardrobe and much more. We have a total of 37 well-appointed rooms at our budget hotel in Kochi.

Relish A Melange Of Flavours

Downtown is a signature multicuisine restaurant at Abad Metro. This restaurant in Kochi serves a range of treats and dishes from across the world onto your platter. Guests who visit our hotel can order a variety of items from our a la carte menu. They can also enjoy an authentic Kerala or South Indian Thali for lunch when they stay at our hotel. All the ingredients that we use for our meals are procured from nearby markets and our chefs work their magic on it to give it a rich and distinct taste. A welcoming ambience exudes from our restaurant and that, combined with our friendly staff, make for a memorable dining experience.

Meetings & Events

Abad Metro has a banquet hall called Summit Banquet Hall which is perfectly suited to host a range of social and business events. This banquet hall in Kochi has an area of 600 sq. ft. and can accommodate up to 65 people. Our banquet hall comes with amenities like air-conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, overhead projector and modern audiovisual equipment.

Some of the social events that you can host at our banquet hall include reunions, birthday parties, anniversaries and business events like annual review meetings, dealer meets, product launches and more.
