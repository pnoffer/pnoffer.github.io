Abad Serviced Villas, Kovalam

Comfortable Stay in Abad Serviced Villas, Kovalam

Abad Serviced Villas in Kovalam offers a perfect stay for guests who wish to go touring in Kerala. It is located 25 minutes away from Trivandrum Central Railway Station and 20 minutes away from Trivandrum International Airport. Ideally positioned on the lighthouse beach, Abad Serviced Villas offers direct access to the beach. Our rooms are spacious, air-conditioned and provide a breathtaking sea view for our guests.

It is conceptually a bed and breakfast stay and we have a caretaker in the property to help you 24 hours a day. English breakfast is served in the morning and we also provide laundry service. It is an excellent option for travellers who would like to stay and explore Kovalam and nearby places!

Accommodation at Abad Serviced Villas, Kovalam

Guests of Abad Serviced Villas can choose between two types of accommodations. Deluxe Room is a spacious 200 sq. ft. accommodation decked with modern amenities. It can accommodate up to 3 guests, 2 adults and 1 child comfortably. This room has a patio that offers ethereal sea views. Superior Room is a bigger room, 320 sq. ft. in size, and can accommodate up to 4 guests, 2 adults and 2 children. They are equipped with modern amenities such as Wi-Fi, LED television, and coffee/tea maker. It has a balcony where guests can settle down and enjoy the soothing sea view!
