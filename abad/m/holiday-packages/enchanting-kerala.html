Itinerary For The Trip

05 NIGHTS AND 06 DAYS


Day01:
Cochin Pick from the airport and proceed to cochin check in hotel after fresh up then proceed to visit, St Francis Church, the oldest Church in India, Cantilevered Chinese fishing nets, 16th-century Synagogue, Jew Town and Mattancherry Palace, built by Portuguese, Palace contains some of India’s most beautiful murals. Then Evening Spend at marine drive. Overnight, stay at Abad Plaza.

Day 02:
Cochin to Munnar After breakfast and proceed to Munnar. Munnar, the most famous hill station situated on the Western Ghats at a height of 1600 meters above sea level. It is essentially a tea town – miles of unending tea plantation can be seen here. En route - Cheyyappara, Valara waterfalls, and Karadipara View Point. Overnight, stay at Abad Copper Castle.

Day 03:
Munnar After breakfast sightseeing, the natural habitat of Nilgiri Thar. Nilgiri Thar can see very closely here. Later visit a tea factory and tea museum, Mattupetty dam, Echo point and Kundala Lake. Boating facilities are available at Mattupetty Dam (entry charges extra payment on your own). Overnight, stay at Abad Copper Castle.

Day 04:
Munnar to Thekkady After breakfast check out from the hotel and departure to Thekkady, which is also known as Periyar Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is one of the world's most fascinating natural wildlife reserves. Enroute visit Spice Plantations etc. Evening for shopping. Overnight at hotel Abad Green Forest.

Day 05:
Thekkady to Kumarakom After breakfast check out from the hotel, depart to Kumarakom.Check-in at the Resort. Visit Kumarakom bird sanctuary and overnight stay at Abad Whispering Palms.

Day 06:
Departure Check out from the hotel, transfer to the Airport and you are your way to the home town.
