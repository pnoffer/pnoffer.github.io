Abad Whispering Palms- A Pristine Resort In Kumarakom

A Mesmeric Lakeside Stay Awaits You at our Kumarakom Resort

Abad Whispering Palms is located on the banks of the pristine Vembanad Lake and offers a perfect escapade for the modern traveller. Set in a beautiful natural environment that comprises a private lake, an infinity swimming pool, and three private plunge pools with a view of the backwaters, our 4-star luxury Kumarakom Lake resorts are the perfect antidote to the stress and anxiety of everyday life. Our resort is located about 83 kilometres from the Cochin International Airport and can be reached within a two-hour drive. Kottayam Railway Station is just 30 minutes from our Kumarakom Resort, Kerala. Guests who visit our resort can choose to stay at one of three living spaces viz., Garden View Rooms, Lake Facing Rooms, and Bamboo Pool Villas. All of our rooms/villas are spacious and are decked out with modern amenities that will help you have a comfortable stay. Our resort features an in-house restaurant called Granary which serves a multitude of delectable delicacies. You can host a range of social and leisure events at Vedhi Banquet Hall. This banquet hall has an area of 2400 sq. ft. and can accommodate 100 people.

Our Kochi resort also arrange stays at our houseboat in the Vembanad lake on request. We have up to 3 bedroom houseboats, which can accommodate 9 guests for overnight stays and up to 25 guests for a day cruise. Honeymoon couples can enjoy their new beginning at our 1 bedroom houseboat. If you want to kick back and relax, you can visit the Revival Ayurvedic Spa, a premier spa present at our resort. This spa in Kumarakom utilizes healing techniques and oil massages from across the world to help you relax and rejuvenate.

When staying at our Kumarakom resort, take time to visit the nearby places like Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, Bay Island Driftwood Museum, Vaikom Mahadev Temple, and many more. 

Wake Up To An Enchanting View Of Nature

Guests who visit our backwater resorts in Kumarakom can stay at our three spacious living spaces viz., Garden View Cottage, Lake View Rooms, and the Bamboo Pool Villa. All of our three rooms come with special features. Garden View Cottage, as the name suggests, overlooks the pristine gardens that are present within our resort. The Lake View Rooms provide an unabated view of the Vembanad Lake and our Bamboo Pool Villas are the epitome of comfort and opulent living as they come with a plunge pool and a private sit-out area. 

Indulge In A Medley Of Flavours

One of the best resorts in Kumarakom, Abad Whispering Palms has an in-house restaurant called the Granary which serves a plethora of multicuisine delicacies. At our restaurant in Kumarakom, we put a strong onus on serving quality and delicious meals for our guests. For that reason, we only procure the freshest vegetables and meats from the nearby markets. The Granary Restaurant accommodates many stalwart chefs who can whip up many delicacies in a jiffy. Come, enjoy a memorable dining experience at our restaurant in Kumarakom. 
 

Host A Variety Of Events

Our resorts near Vembanad Lake also accommodates a 2400 sq. ft. banquet hall called Vedhi that can accommodate a crowd of about 100 people. This banquet hall comes with amenities such as air-conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, overhead projector and modern audiovisual equipment. Our banquet hall is perfectly suited to host both business and leisure events with grace. Some of the leisure events you can host at our banquet hall include birthday parties, reunions, get-togethers and business events like product launches, corporate retreats, annual review meetings and much more.
