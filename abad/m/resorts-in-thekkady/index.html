Abad Green Forest- Resorts near Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

An Impeccable Vacationing Experience Awaits You at the Best Resort in Thekkady

Abad Green Forest is an idyllic resort that borders the Periyar wildlife sanctuary and provides the perfect antidote to the busy lives of modern travellers. Our resort in Thekkady is located about 161 kilometres from the Cochin International Airport and has an average travel time of four hours and twenty minutes. You can avail taxis and private vehicles to reach our hotel from the airport. Guests who visit our hotel can stay at our Jungle Cottages and Jungle Rooms, which offer a perfect sojourn. Our resort in Thekkady accommodates an in-house restaurant called Peppermint where you can enjoy an array of succulent meals from across the world. A 1470 sq. ft. banquet hall also graces our resort and it can accommodate up to 75 people. Our banquet halls are the perfect place to host a variety of small events like birthday parties, anniversaries, corporate retreats, workshops, and more. A natural spa called Revival Ayurveda centre is also present at our resort where you can enjoy a plethora of massages and can top it off with a sauna experience. 

When you stay at our picturesque resort in Thekkady, take time to visit the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, spice walk, Chellarkovil waterfalls, and many other tourist destinations that are present in close proximity to our resort. 

Your ideal vacation begins at Abad Green Forest, the best resort in Thekkady!

Wake Up To The Magical Rhapsody Of Nature

Guests who stay at our resort in Thekkady can choose to stay at our Jungle Cottages or Jungle Rooms. These rooms in Thekkady can accommodate 3 adults and 2 children comfortably. These rooms are standalone structures which are both spacious and offer the perfect solitude to our guests. Some Jungle Rooms even have an interconnecting door between the rooms for easier access. This feature is particularly useful if you are vacationing with a large group of friends or family. Some of the amenities that are available at our rooms include air-conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, a mini bar, tea & coffee maker, and laundry service. Have a great time at our jungle resort in Thekkady!

Delve Into A Plethora of Succulent Flavours

Abad Green Forest has an in-house restaurant called Peppermint. Our restaurant in Thekkady, located by the poolside, serves a plethora of succulent dishes that will take your tastebuds on an adventure. All the ingredients that are used in our restaurant are freshly sourced and thus add to the existing taste of the food. Guests who stay at our hotel can enjoy breakfast, lunch and dinner options at our restaurant. We also have many veteran chefs who can customize the dishes to meet your varied needs.

Come, enjoy a redefined dining experience at our resort in Thekkady!

Host Regal Events

At Abad Green Forest, we have a 1470 sq. ft. banquet hall which can accommodate up to 75 people and is the perfect place to host a variety of leisure and corporate events. The speciality of our banquet hall is that it is an open hall, meaning you can enjoy an unobstructed view of the event from around the hall. Our banquet hall comes with a host of amenities like an overhead projector, modern audio-visual equipment, and high-speed Wi-Fi. Some of the events you can host at the banquet halls at our Thekkady resort include birthday parties, anniversaries, get-togethers, workshops, corporate retreats, and boot camps.
