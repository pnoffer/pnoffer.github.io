Abad Brookside Lakkidi, Wayanad

Welcome to Abad Brookside Lakkidi - One of the Best Resorts in Wayanad

Abad Brookside is a rain themed resort, set in the backdrop of one of the most beautiful places in Kerala. Nestled amidst the lush green mountains and misty landscapes of Wayanad, Abad Brookside Lakkidi offers the perfect getaway from the city humdrum. The resort is surrounded by the dense forest of the Western Ghats, lush green mountains, and misty landscapes, making it one of the best resorts in Wayanad for guests to experience nature and a peaceful stay. Our Wayanad resort is a property with 30 rooms spread out in a seven-acre valley with an infinity pool, Ayurveda spa and wellness centre, and a multi-cuisine restaurant.  Each cottage at our resort has a balcony or sit-out that provides an excellent view of the adjoining forest. A brook runs through the edge of the resort. Abad Brookside has an outdoor infinity pool with a beautiful view of the forest. Adventurer lovers get to boost their adrenaline rush by indulging in various activities in and around our resort in Lakkidi like forest exploration, trekking, ziplining, cycling in the hills, campfire, etc. This is the perfect vacationing spot you were always craving for!

Live Amidst Nature in Wayanad!

To enjoy the tranquil beauty of Wayanad, there can be no better place than Abad Brookside Lakkidi. We house accommodations in two types of cottages that are customized for different experiences and offer the best stay in Wayanad. The splendidly furnished Garden Facing Cottage has a balcony where you can relax and soak up the rustic beauty of Wayanad. The Brookside Cottage has a large bedroom, a living space, and a balcony. It has a brook running by its side and the fresh smell of the rainwashed forest sweeps into these rooms, giving you comfort and peace! These rooms in Wayanad have plenty of space to use and are equipped with all modern amenities.

Tantalize Your Taste Buds In Our Multi-Cuisine Restaurant!

Abad Brookside Lakkidi has an in-house multi-cuisine restaurant. It serves delicacies from all over the world onto your platter, and our skilful chefs are experienced in customizing it to meet our guests' taste and requirements. Quality, taste, and satisfaction are the three biggest keywords in our restaurant and we aim at fulfilling the commitments by treating our guests with a mouth-watering spread. Our friendly staff is always here to serve you with a smile and help you have a delectable dining experience! Buffet is also available on special occasions!

Meetings and Events

 

We feature a banquet hall in Wayanad. It is spread over an area of 1783 sq. ft. and is capable of entertaining more than a hundred guests. With amenities such as projectors and audiovisual equipment, our banquet hall in Wayanad is more than enough for conducting business events, birthday parties and kitty parties!
