Exciting Holiday Packages to Enthralling Locations!

 

Abad Hotels and resorts have been delighting travellers from the world over with holiday experiences to suit your individual taste and budget in Kerala. We offer you customised and personalised holiday packages with a wide range of sightseeing tours in hill stations, near wildlife sanctuaries, backwaters, beaches and more.

Stay in a 350-year-old heritage hotel or a floating accommodation in the backwaters or amidst a 4000-acre tea plantation. Our holiday packages offer other wide range of experiences like a fisherman’s village visit while staying at our eco-friendly beach resort in Marari or go on a trek with bird watching at our rainforest resort in Wayanad or stay in a hotel perched on a cliff overlooking a valley in Munnar. Our tours also include a day tour from Kovalam to Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of India to watch the sunset.
