      Deluxe Room

      Deluxe rooms at Abad Metro are spacious and are perfect for both leisure and business travellers. Our rooms in Kochi have an area of 300 sq. ft. and can accommodate 2 adults and 2 children comfortably. The Deluxe rooms are larger than the Standard rooms and come with comfortable single/twin beds where you can rest and relax after a long day. Some of the amenities available at our rooms include air-conditioning, attached bathroom, wardrobe,  LED television and more.
