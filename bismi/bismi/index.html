Welcome! Bismi

BISMI, a name often chanted by the majority of housewives in Kerala, has a story of success that rolls back to 2003. Bismi was known to be the pioneer in successfully giving the first innovative and elite experience for the local crowd in buying white goods.

Bismi is currently the unbeatable stalwarts in Kerala flaunting numerous hypermarkets, white goods and electronic retail stores.
