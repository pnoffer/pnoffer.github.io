Romantic Dinner by the Shores
What's included

Experience a private dining with your loved one like nowhere else. Start off the intimate celebration with a magnificent sunset view from Crown Island with your toes dipped in the sand. Top off the night with an utterly romantic meal with your partner with a table for two in a candlelit tent by the glittering beach, taking in the view of the night sky. Choose from gourmet menus or design your own in collaboration with your chef for the evening.
